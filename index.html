Training Programs

Prep Programs

In our preparation program, we offer a step-by-step guide to building a strong foundation in ball control, dribbling, passing, individual defense, footwork, and key shooting skills.

Junior Programs

As a dynamic and high-intensity sport, basketball requires athletes to perform skills intermittently. In this program, we focus on next-level technical skills, such as deceptive ball handling to outmaneuver defenders, various shooting techniques, passing drills to create openings, rebounding with proper box-out techniques, and incorporating fake maneuvers and feints.

Specialist Program

Introducing our Specialist Programs tailored to enhance the skills of specific player positions. The "COMBO Guard Curl" program is designed for Point Guards and Shooting Guards, focusing on refining their versatile playing style. For the big centers looking to elevate their game, we present the "BIGMAN Boost" program, strategically crafted to meet the unique demands of the center position. Additionally, our "SWING MAN BOX" program caters to the needs of combo Shooting Guards and Small Forwards, offering a comprehensive training regimen to excel in both scoring and defensive roles. Elevate your game with our specialized programs, designed to bring out the best in each player's position-specific abilities.

Hoops Fitness Program

In this program, unique workouts are carefully curated to cater to both on-court and off-court training needs. Whether you're working with or without a ball, our program is designed to enhance various aspects of your game. Focus on improving Speed Endurance, Speed & Agility, Strength & Power, as well as Core Strength & Injury Prevention. Elevate your basketball performance with our holistic approach to fitness that addresses key elements crucial for success on the court.

Meticulous Shooting Program

In this program, focus on shooting form & technique, shooting in rhythm, shooting under various situations, and different shooting structures.
